## Review Status Values

- `Not started`
- `Under review`
- `Revise and resubmit`
- `Approved with comments`
- `Approved`
- `Superseded`

## Comment Quality Standard

Each review comment should include:

- Location in the document.
- Issue or ambiguity.
- Required correction.
- Impact on safety, reliability, schedule, or handover evidence.
